Arnaldo Carvalho de Melo is a Brazilian developer working on the Linux kernel, where he is the primary maintainer of the DCCP implementation,[1][failed verification] and is also involved in other development work[specify]. He's also very known for his extensive work on the Linux command perf.

Work[edit]

De Melo was one of the key founders of Conectiva which later became part of Mandriva[citation needed].
