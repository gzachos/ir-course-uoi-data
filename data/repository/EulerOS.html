EulerOS is a commercial Linux distribution developed by Huawei Technologies based on CentOS for enterprise applications.[1] It is one of five commercial operating systems that have versions certified to The Open Group's UNIX 03 standard (the others being macOS, Solaris, HP-UX, and IBM AIX).[2]

Huawei has released a community edition of EulerOS, OpenEuler, along with the source code at Gitee.[3]
