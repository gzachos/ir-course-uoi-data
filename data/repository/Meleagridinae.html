Meleagridinae is a subfamily of birds in the family Phasianidae. It includes turkeys and their extinct relatives.

Systematics[edit]

  • Genus Meleagris
    • Wild turkey Meleagris gallopavo
      • Domesticated turkey
    • Ocellated turkey Meleagris ocellata
    • Californian turkey Meleagris californica †[1][2]
    • Meleagris crassipes †[3]
  • Genus Proagriocharis †
    • Proagriocharis kimballensis †[4][5]
  • Genus Rhegminornis †
    • Rhegminornis calobates †[6][7][8]
