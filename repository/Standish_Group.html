The Standish Group International, Inc. or Standish Group is an independent international IT research advisory firm founded in 1985,[1] known from their reports about information systems implementation projects in the public and private sector.[2] The firm focuses on mission-critical software applications, especially focussing on failures and possible improvements in IT projects. The Standish Group has reported on subjects such as software: commercial, free, and open source; project management such as critical chain project management; projects such as Confirm Project, etc.
